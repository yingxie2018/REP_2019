• The “Enrolment Status”, “Enrolment Version”, and “Date Last Saved” fields are automatically filled by the form upon selecting “Save Working Copy” or “Save Draft for Filing” in the bottom of the template.
  • The “Control Number” field will be filled by Health Canada when generating final xml file.
  • The “Amend Enrolment” button is only enabled when a Final Regulatory Activity XML is loaded into the template, and must be selected when amending a Final Regulatory Activity XML.
  • The “Company ID” can be obtained from the final company XML file, finalized by Health Canada.
  • The “Dossier ID” can be obtained from the final dossier XML file, or other Health Canada issued document. (e.g. HC6-024-x######, i.e. HC6-024-e172345).
  • The “Amend Enrolment” button must be selected when amending a final regulatory activity XML. Load the final XML file in the template to enable the “Amend Enrolment” button.
